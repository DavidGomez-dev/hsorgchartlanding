# Unlocking Better Deal Qualification in HubSpot Sales with Org Charts

In the fast-paced world of B2B sales, deal qualification is everything. One of the most underutilized tools that can revolutionize your HubSpot sales process is the humble but powerful org chart. Let’s explore how org charts improve deal qualification in HubSpot, and how you can leverage them for more efficient selling.

---

## What is Deal Qualification in HubSpot Sales?

Deal qualification in HubSpot sales means evaluating prospects to ensure they’re a good fit, have the budget, and the authority to buy. Good qualification prevents sales teams from wasting time pursuing dead-end opportunities. This process helps you focus on high-potential leads, improving your close rates and boosting your funnel efficiency.

---

## The Role of Org Charts in Deal Qualification

Org charts, or organizational charts, visualize the hierarchy and relationships within a target company. When integrated into your HubSpot sales workflow, they bring three crucial advantages:

### 1. Identifying Decision Makers and Influencers

An org chart maps out every key player in your target account. You can instantly spot:

- The economic decision maker
- Gatekeepers
- Champions
- Influencers

By seeing these roles at a glance, your deal qualification becomes smarter. You won’t waste time selling to the wrong contact—HubSpot users can attach org charts directly to contact and deal records for quick reference.

### 2. Navigating Complex Buying Groups

B2B deals often involve multiple stakeholders. Org charts help HubSpot sales reps:

- Understand reporting structures
- Track buying committees
- Pinpoint internal advocates

With this clarity, you can tailor messages and strategies to each stakeholder, dramatically improving your chances of progressing the deal.

### 3. Forecasting and Risk Management

Not all risks are obvious. An org chart highlights:

- Gaps in your relationship within the account
- Missing information on key roles
- Areas where competitors have stronger ties

With these insights, your deal qualification in HubSpot is driven by data, not guesswork.

---

## How to Use Org Charts in HubSpot Sales

Ready to integrate org charts into your HubSpot sales process? Here’s a quick guide:

1. **Build the Org Chart:** Use LinkedIn, company websites, and direct conversations to outline the company structure.
2. **Attach Org Charts to HubSpot Records:** Upload images or use HubSpot partnerships (like wizard tools or Chrome extensions) to keep org charts handy.
3. **Tag Key Roles:** Mark each stakeholder as champion, influencer, decision maker, or blocker.
4. **Regularly Update:** As you learn more, refine the org chart. This keeps your deal qualification accurate and up to date.
